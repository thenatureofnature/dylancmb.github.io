About Me

I am an astrophysicist and cosmologist, dedicated to understanding the fundamental laws that govern our universe. My research focuses on dark energy, dark matter, and the large-scale structure of the cosmos.

Currently, I am a student at Central department of physics, Tribhuvan University, where I use data from galaxy surveys and advanced computational methods to probe the mysteries of the universe.

Research Interests

Cosmic Voids

Exploring the vast, empty regions of space to understand the large-scale structure of the Universe and its implications for cosmology.

Variable Stars

I use statistical tools to understand the big data obtained from satellite experiments to understand the variability of these stars .

Galaxy Surveys

Analyzing data from large-scale galaxy surveys to map the distribution of matter in the Universe and constrain cosmological parameters.
